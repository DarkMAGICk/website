THE WORLD OF DARKMAGICK

You are about to enter into a world of full of magick, monsters and demons. But you we're born to become a great magician, born to tap into your higher-self and bring balance into your soul. Your village needs you, the world needs you. What do you do?

DarkMagick RPG is a action style RPG built on Binance Smart Chain and offering a great play to earn mechanism. Players of DarkMagick can earn $DMGK tokens and NFTs by completing quests and tasks in the game. DarkMagick is built with Unity3D to give players the most high-end gameplay experience. We have reached MVP with our Hero Mint NFT dapplication and we are currently developing our Alpha to release in Q4 2021.

DarkMagick Gamez Ltd Co is a registered LLC in USA.


HERO MINT

Hero Mint allows players to mint their own 1:1 NFT Hero to play with in the game. Which means no one else can ever create the same Hero avatar as you again. We are the first game ever to offer players a truly non-fungible gaming experience.



P2E BASED ON XP & NFT RARITY

DarkMagick has a unique p2e model that is based on XP points earned while playing the game. XP points are earned by completing tasks/quests, defeating enemies and boss characters. Discover and create new NFTs to stake and earn $DMGK in the Magick Staking System



MAGICK STAKING

Our Magick Staking System is one-of-a-kind and will reward players based on their in-game efforts.By staking NFTs, completing tasks, and earning XP players will receive $DMGK rewards directly to their wallet. The more rare the NFT you stake, the bigger the reward.


DUNGEON CRAWLING

Enter through the red portal gates to descend through different levels and battle countless enemies. Players will gain XP and NFT collectibles as they make their way through the dungeon.


HERO METAVERSE

Enter through the blue portal gates and meet up with friends online. Build a strategy together and go battle through the dungeon crawl as a team collecting NFTs and earning XP.


Game Highlights

Solo Story Mode

Travel down the tree of life map through 10 magickal levels. After a level is complete, receive a sephirot NFT. Players will need to combine all the sephirot NFT in order to reveal the final level and become a Dark Magican!

PvP & Dungeon Mode

• Bet against other players to earn $DMGK, $BNB and NFT
• Bet your NFT Hero against other players, winner takes all!
• Battle through unlimited levels of dungeons fighting monsters, mages, skeletons, demons and magicians earning XP and looting NFT items

NFT Alchemy

Become an NFT alchemist and combine your collectibles to create new more rare collectibles. It won't be a simple task though, the NFT alchemy station can only be access from the middle sephirot on the tree of life, and you must play the game and find the portal that leads you there.
